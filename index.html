Hi. I'm Rick Scheibner.

I am a visual media artist specializing in music, sports and portraiture throughout the NE Oregon/SE Washington region. I have a passion for creating timeless images that are both both classic and contemporary, without being trendy. Whether you're a graduating senior, a young athlete, or a musician who wants to take your work to the next level, the photos we create will look spectacular today and for years to come.
