Advanced Color

L*a*b* Adjustments Panel

The secret weapon of RawTherapee. L*a*b* separates Luminosity from Color, allowing you to boost saturation or change brightness without shifting the hue.

  • LH (Lightness vs Hue): Make specific colors (like yellow flowers) pop against dark backgrounds.
  • CH (Chromaticity vs Hue): Selectively saturate or desaturate specific color ranges.
  • HH (Hue vs Hue): Shift one hue toward another—perfect for correcting skin tones.

Precision Detail

Capture Sharpening

Found in the Raw Tab, this tool corrects the softness introduced by your camera's sensor and lens before any further processing occurs. It provides much finer detail than standard Unsharp Masking.

Tip: Always view at 1:1 Zoom (100%) to see the true effect of sharpening and noise reduction.

Noise Reduction

Utilize the Impulse Noise Reduction to eliminate "salt and pepper" artifacts and the powerful Luminance/Chrominance sliders to clean up high-ISO shots without losing textures.

Pro Workflow Tips

Speed Up Your Editing

Create a base.pp3 profile. This profile should include your default corrections like Chromatic Aberration and Lens Profiles. Set it to auto-apply in Preferences > Image Processing.

Preferences -> Image Processing -> Default Processing Profile

Batch Processing

Don't wait for every image to export. Add them to the Queue (shortcut: Ctrl+Q or B) and process them all at once when you're finished editing.
